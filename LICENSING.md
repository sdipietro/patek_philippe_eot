## Status: UNLICENSED — do not publish this site without replacing or licensing every image listed below.

The watch photographs in `public/watches/` were sourced from public auction-house catalogues and the Huber & Banbery reference plates. They are reproduced here for development purposes only. Each image must be replaced with a licensed substitute, or written permission must be obtained from the rights holder, before any public deployment.

## Required actions before launch

1. Contact each auction house (Sotheby's, Christie's, Antiquorum, Phillips, Bonhams, Dobiaschofsky, Cortrie) to request a licence or written permission to reproduce catalogue photographs in a non-commercial research archive.
2. For Huber & Banbery archival plates, contact the publisher and the Patek Philippe Museum.
3. Alternatively, commission new photography for all in-collection examples (111505 at Beyer Museum, 866721 at Musée d'Horlogerie du Locle, 198023 formerly at Smithsonian).
4. Add a `photoCredit` field to the relevant records in `lib/records.ts` once rights are confirmed. Render it as an italic ink-400 caption beneath the lightbox image on the detail page.
5. Update this file to reflect the final licensing status of each image.
